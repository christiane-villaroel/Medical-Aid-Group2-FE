About Empr Medical

Empr Medical ‘s mission is to provide high-quality clinical patient care to the people of Louisiana, Mississippi, and Texas. Established in 2019, our healthcare locations are comprised of than 500 employees and 1,000 providers in over 90 medical specialties and subspecialties. We are driven by our commitment to our mission to Serve, Heal, Lead, Educate, and Innovate modern healthcare systems in the US and beyond.
